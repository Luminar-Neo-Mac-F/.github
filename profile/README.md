## 💡 Overview

<a href="#luminarNeo">Luminar Neo</a> is Skylum's AI-powered photo editing platform that emphasizes creative transformation over technical correction — providing tools that reimagine image content, enhance portraits with machine learning, and dramatically alter the mood and atmosphere of photographs.

The <a href="#luminarNeo">Sky AI replacement tool</a> detects and replaces the sky in any photo with a selected sky from the built-in library or custom sky images — intelligently relighting the foreground elements to match the new sky's lighting conditions, adjusting reflections in water, and blending the replacement convincingly. What would require hours of skilled manual compositing in Photoshop completes automatically. The <a href="#luminarNeo">Relight Scene AI tool</a> adjusts the lighting in a photograph as if from a virtual light source in 3D space — brightening foreground subjects while maintaining background lighting, adding fill light to shadow areas, and simulating changed lighting setup during post-processing.

The <a href="#luminarNeo">Portrait AI suite</a> applies machine learning to portrait enhancement — Face AI refines facial features and removes blemishes; Skin AI applies adaptive skin retouching; Body AI provides body shape enhancement; Eye AI enhances eye detail. The <a href="#luminarNeo">Bokeh AI tool</a> adds or enhances background blur — detecting the subject from background and applying algorithmically computed lens blur to create the shallow depth-of-field appearance regardless of the original lens used.</p>

<p align="center">

<a href="#luminarNeo">Atmosphere AI</a> adds realistic fog, mist, and haze effects to photos — synthesizing volumetric atmospheric effects that add mood and depth in ways standard overlays cannot replicate. <a href="#luminarNeo">Structure AI</a> enhances texture and detail while protecting skin surfaces from over-sharpening — applying structure enhancement based on image content rather than uniformly.

<a href="#luminarNeo">Layers and masking system</a> provides compositing infrastructure for complex edits — multiple adjustment layers with masks for targeted effects and non-destructive workflow. <a href="#luminarNeo">Lightroom and Photoshop plugin integration</a> runs Luminar Neo as a plugin within those applications — AI tools in the existing editing workflow without restructuring around a separate application.</p>
